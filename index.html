Money Race

roll the die, buy things that pay you, quit the rat race

You get a random job. You don't pick your starting point in real life either. Then you move around the board grabbing assets, things that pay you, until they cover your bills every month. That's the whole game.

Most people never get taught this stuff in school. Money Race is a free browser game where every card, every deal, and every setback maps to a real personal finance idea, cashflow, compound interest, good debt versus bad debt, diversification, and what actually counts as an asset. No signup, no download, just open it and play.
